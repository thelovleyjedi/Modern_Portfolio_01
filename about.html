BIO

Hello! My name is Kingsley Lewis. I graduated from DeVry University with a bachelor’s degree in Computer Science in 2012. I started out in sales and working directly with customers. I eventually secured a position at Ryder Logistics as a Logistics Coordinator in 2015. My career started off there by building an access database for the Richelieu account. The database was linked to an external Excel spreadsheet that was extracted from a program Ryder provided. Within the access database, I was able to write multiple SQL queries that in pulled order numbers, customer names, and shipment orders into an Excel file. I then moved on to execution(building,planning, tendering loads). The last title I held at Ryder Logistics was the White Glove Specialist. The position required company-wide high-level tracking and reporting for the Dr.Pepper account. The data had to be updated by refreshing it in Tableau and publishing it. I continue to keep abreast with technological changes with courses on Youtube, Udemy, and W3schools. I honestly want to make a career change to help others in a similar situation learn to code like me.
